### ✍️ The Visionary Behind the Tools
I am an **English Honours Graduate** who chose to swap the pen for a keyboard—or rather, for **AI-driven development**. My journey is unique: I don't come from a traditional CS background, but I believe that technology should be accessible and localized. 

I use Artificial Intelligence as my primary engineering partner to build high-performance web applications that solve real-world problems for the Indian community.

---

### 🚀 My Flagship Project: [OmixCalculator](https://omixcalculator.in)
**OmixCalculator** is my mission to build the ultimate "Calculator Hub for India." While global tools exist, they often miss the specific nuances of the Indian academic and career system. I’m changing that.

*   **Custom-Built for India:** Dedicated tools for university-specific grading (CGPA/SGPA) and competitive exam logistics (SSC, Banking, UPSC).
*   **AI-Engineered:** A showcase of how modern AI can empower non-traditional developers to build enterprise-grade software.
*   **Precision Focused:** Every tool is data-driven and verified for the Indian landscape.
